# PRD: Recovery & Biometrics

> **ID:** 18.3 | **Status:** Shipped | **Priority:** P0
> **Last Updated:** 2026-04-04

---

## Purpose

Integrate biometric data from Apple Watch (HealthKit) and smart scales to calculate daily readiness scores, enabling users to make informed decisions about training intensity and recovery.

## Business Objective

Readiness scoring is the "glue" between training and nutrition — it answers "should I train hard today?" This cross-feature signal drives the AI recommendation engine and differentiates FitMe from single-purpose competitors.

## Target Persona(s)

- **Primary:** The Data-Driven Optimizer (monitors HRV, RHR, sleep for performance)
- **Secondary:** The Consistent Lifter (wants to know when to push and when to rest)

## Current Implementation

### Data Sources
| Source | Metrics | Method |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Apple Watch via HealthKit | Heart rate, resting HR, HRV, VO2Max, steps, active calories, sleep (total/deep/REM) | Live sync with background delivery |
| Xiaomi S400 Smart Scale | Weight, body fat %, lean mass, muscle mass, bone mass, visceral fat, body water %, BMI, metabolic age, BMR | Manual entry form |
| Manual fallback | Resting HR, HRV, sleep hours | For users without Apple Watch |

### Readiness Scoring Algorithm
- **Formula:** 40% HRV + 30% RHR + 30% Sleep quality → 0-100 score
- **RHR thresholds:** <60 excellent → <75 good → <85 caution → alert
- **HRV thresholds:** ≥45 excellent → ≥35 good → ≥28 caution → alert
- **isReadyForTraining:** RHR < 75 AND HRV ≥ 28

### Screens
| Screen | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| ManualBiometricEntry | Sheet with scale readings (weight, BF%) and manual overrides (RHR, HRV, sleep) |
| ReadinessCard (Page 0) | Readiness score with HRV, RHR, Sleep breakdown |
| Metrics tiles on Home | HRV, RHR, Sleep, Steps quick-view |

### Key Features
- Live HealthKit sync with 500ms debounce (handles 4-6 observer callbacks post-workout)
- Historical data fetching (7/14/30/90 day trends)
- Source badges (Apple Health, Smart Scale, Body Check-ins, Manual Recovery)
- Color-coded status indicators per metric
- Configurable HR zone thresholds for Zone 2 training
