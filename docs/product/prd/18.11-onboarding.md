# PRD: Onboarding

> **ID:** 18.11 | **Status:** Planned (Not Yet Built) | **Priority:** P1
> **Last Updated:** 2026-04-04

---

## Purpose

Guide new users through app setup — account creation, goal selection, HealthKit permissions, and first-action orientation — to maximize D1 retention and reduce time-to-value.

## Business Objective

Onboarding is the highest-leverage retention intervention. Industry benchmarks show that users who complete onboarding have 2-3x higher D7 retention. A well-designed onboarding flow also drives HealthKit authorization (critical for readiness scoring) and goal setting (critical for AI recommendations).

## Target Persona(s)

- **All new users**

## Planned Implementation

### Flow (3-5 Screens)
1. **Welcome** — FitMe logo, tagline, "Get Started" CTA
2. **Goals** — Select primary goal: Build Muscle / Lose Fat / Maintain / General Fitness
3. **Profile** — Basic info: age range, training experience, training frequency
4. **Permissions** — HealthKit authorization (HR, HRV, steps, sleep) + notifications
5. **First Action** — Direct to today's training session or meal logging
